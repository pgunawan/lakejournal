Dig Your Neighborhood

Lake Publishing Society (LPS) supports a University of British Columbia initiative that links student artists with the local community- the DIG YOUR NEIGHBOURHOOD project. Student artists create literary and artistic works directly related to specific Kelowna neighbourhoods and, through a specially created imprint, DYN, LPS publishes these works which are sold in the community and delivered free to newcomers by the Welcome Wagon Ltd.
