Dig Your Neighborhood

Lake Publishing Society (LPS) supports a University of British Columbia initiative that links student artists with the local community- the DIG YOUR NEIGHBOURHOOD project. Student artists create literary and artistic works directly related to specific Kelowna neighbourhoods and, through a specially created imprint, DYN, LPS publishes these works which are sold in the community and delivered free to newcomers by the Welcome Wagon Ltd. Watch for the April Launch of DYN Mission! For more information see our page.
