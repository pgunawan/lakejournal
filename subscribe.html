LAKE IS NO LONGER ACCEPTING SUBSCRIPTIONS.

As you may or may not have heard, Lake Issue #8 will be the last print issue published by the Lake Publishing Society. We at Lake have enjoyed working with contributors and readers alike and it was with heavy hearts that we decided to cease publication. We still believe there is a place for Lake, in fact, now more than ever. Lake Publishing Society will continue to exist and we look forward to publishing some limited edition works on the theme of art and environment. It is possible that we may continue with some kind of online presence with our website, as well, including publishing reviews and some artwork.

In lieu of these changes, we at Lake hope to digitize our archives (issues 1 – 7 as well as 8 upon completion) in the UBCO cIRcle database which can be toured here:    https://circle.ubc.ca/handle/2429/43542

If you pre-paid for a subscription beyond issue 8, you are entitled to a refund. Please contact Lake at lake.journal@ubc.ca for further details. You may, however, choose to have a back issue of Lake (issues 1-7) sent in lieu of monetary refund, if preferred.
If you have any further questions please contact the Lake office.

Thank you
